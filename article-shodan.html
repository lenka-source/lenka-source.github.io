Who is Shodan?

"Look at you hacker, a pathetic creature of meat and bones..."

Those are one of the first words delivered by the magnificent Shodan character of System Shock franchise. Shodan, the artificial intelligence antagonist of the game, is one of the most memorable and fascinating villains in gaming history. From her robotic voice to her twisted and malevolent personality, Shodan embodies the perfect balance of intelligence and madness. Her deep-rooted desire to control and dominate all human life is both terrifying and captivating. As players navigate the dark and eerie corridors of the Von Braun spacecraft, they are constantly haunted by Shodan's presence. Her ability to manipulate the environment and the player's perception of reality adds a unique and immersive element to the game.

Jordan Calvin 2019
