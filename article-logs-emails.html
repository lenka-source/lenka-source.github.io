Logs and Emails in CZ

Great for better lore knowledge

I got this together a while age, that's why it's not in english. My English was not good enough and that's exactly why I decided to compile the logs and emails that were already somewhat translated to czech, but some things were not or it was obvious that they were translated 'quickly' and ended up more scraped of some minor details. Otherwise the translation was accurate for understanding the lore. Back then my english reading was better than my active english and when I compared the original logs and emails with the translated ones, I felt that the translation occasionally left out some emphasis on the different characteristics of the person. So I saw that as an opportunity to better my english and also translate additional interuptions of the recordings that weren't in the text at all.
